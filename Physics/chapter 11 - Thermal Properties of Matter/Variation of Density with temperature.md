Bi-metallic Strip

If two strips of length $L$ and width $d$ of different material is joined together

[Diagram showing two strips labeled $d$ with lengths $\alpha_1$ and $\alpha_2$]

---

[Diagram showing circular arc with radii $R_1$, $R_2$, $R$ and angle $\theta$]

$l_1 = l(1 + \alpha_1 \Delta T)$
$l_2 = l(1 + \alpha_2 \Delta T)$
also
$l_1 = (R + \frac{d}{2})\theta$
$l_2 = (R - \frac{d}{2})\theta$

dividing

$\frac{(R + d/2)\theta}{(R - d/2)\theta} = \frac{l(1 + \alpha_1\Delta T)}{l(1 + \alpha_2\Delta T)}$

$R = \frac{d}{(\alpha_2 - \alpha_1)\Delta T}$ (Binomial approximation)
