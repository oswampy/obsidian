Cyclic Process

In cyclic process, initial and final states are same

$P_i = P_f$, $V_i = V_f$, $T_i = T_f$

Yet it is not isothermal, isobaric or isochoric because in cyclic the pressure can go up and then come back to same value of $P_i$ but in isobaric pressure doesn't change throughout the process

In cyclic process $\Delta u$ will be $0$ because it is dir prop to $T_i$ and $T_f$
