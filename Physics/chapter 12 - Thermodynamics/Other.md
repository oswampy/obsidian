Idealized, infinitely slow process where
a system and its surrounding can
return to its exact initial state by
reversing the process with no net
change to the universe and maintaining
equilibrium throughout.

work done in rev process is called
reversible work done

slowly ensuring system is always
in eqm with surrounding
