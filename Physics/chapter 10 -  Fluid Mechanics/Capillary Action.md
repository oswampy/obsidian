

# Image 1: Capillary Effect

**Capillary Effect**

A glass tube with small diameter is called capillary.

If we dip a capillary tube in water, pressure just below surface will be less than pressure above surface.

	![[capillary.excalidraw]]

$P_i = P_o - \frac{2T}{R_c} + \frac{T}{3}gh$

$P = P_o$

$\frac{P}{-2T} + \frac{R_c}{3}gh = P_i$

$$h = \frac{2T}{3gR_c}$$

relation between radius of capillary (r) and Radius of curvature of surface (Rc)

$Rc = \frac{r}{\cos\theta}$

So $h = \frac{2T\cos\theta}{3gr}$
