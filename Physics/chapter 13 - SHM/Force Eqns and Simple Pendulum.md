

### Steps to solve Q₁ using Force Method

1. Identify forces, Draw FBD
2. Find eq^m pos where Fnet = 0
3. Displace the object by small distance but not to extreme position
4. Calculate new net force acting on the object considering all forces
5. Apply Newton's laws of motion to ensure net force in form of acceleration
6. Write the eq^n of m in form of differential equation
7. Compare this eq^n with standard differential equation of SHM and find ω.

## Simple Pendulum

• An Ideal Simple Pendulum have heavy point mass, inextensible and perfectly flexible string hanging in frictionless medium and oscillating in a plane

• Point from which Pendulum is suspended is called point of suspension (POS)

• The center of mass of system which coincides with COM of BOB (Point of oscillation)

• Effective length = dist between point of suspension and point of oscillation

In Simple pendulum Torque and α will be calculated about POS
