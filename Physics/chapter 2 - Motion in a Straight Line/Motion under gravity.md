### Time of Flight:
when ball is thrown upwards:
$\vec{u} = u$
$\vec{v} = v$
$\vec{v} = -u$
in equation
$-u = -u -gt$
$gt = 2u$
$t = \frac{2u}{g}$

in time of ascent, v is 0 because it stops moving upwards after reaching its peak
$0 = u -gt$
$t_{a}=u/g$

where t = $t_{a} +t_{d}$ (time of ascent and descent)
$t_{a}=t_{d} = \frac{u}{g}$
Total height:
$H = \frac{u^2}{2g}$

### Velocity under gravity
$H = \frac{1}{2}gt^2$
$t = \sqrt{2h/g}$
$\vec{v} = \sqrt{2gh}$
