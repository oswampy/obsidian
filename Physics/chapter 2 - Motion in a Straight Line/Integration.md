Integration is used for finding the total area under a curve. it sums up smaller areas to get an accurate figure. Integrals are used to solve problems where quantities are changing continuously

there are 2 types of integrals -> definite and indefinite
- Definite integrals have specified limits (bounds) and yield a single numerical value.
- Indefinite integrals result in a function plus a constant(C).

## Important formulas
$$\int x^ndx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C  [n\ne 1]$$
$$\int \frac{1}{x} dx = ln|x| + C$$
$$\int e^x dx = e^x +C $$
$$\int \sin xdx = -\cos x+C $$
$$\int \cos xdx = \sin x + C $$
