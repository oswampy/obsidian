- In mechanics, constraints are defines as limitations or restriction to motion of any particle or system of particles.
- Ex-
	1) a pendulum; the distance of bob of pendulum from hanging point cannot be more than length of attached string
	2) Distance between two blocks connected by a rod of length L is always L
	3) An object moving on ground is constrainted to move above ground
