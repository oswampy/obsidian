## Electrostatics

**Charge** is a property of a body due to which it can create electric and magnetic effects in its surrounding.

- **SI unit:** coulomb (C)
    
- **CGS unit:** esu (electrostatic unit)
    
- **Dimension:** $[IT]$ or $[AT]$
    

---

### Properties

- **Scalar:** It has magnitude but no direction.
    
- **Transferrable:** Charge can be transferred from one body to another.
    
- **Always associated with mass:** Charge cannot exist without mass, though mass can exist without net charge.
    
- **Like charges repel, unlike attract:** Fundamental law of electrostatics.
    
- **Charges are quantised ($q = ne$):** The total charge of a body is an integral multiple of the basic unit of charge (electron charge).
    
- **Charge doesn't depend on reference frame:** The magnitude of charge is invariant to the velocity of the observer.
