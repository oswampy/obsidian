
## Standard limits:

1. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1$ (has to be in $\frac{0}{0}$ form, not necessarily approach 0)
    
2. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan x}{x} = 1$ 
    
3. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin^{-1}x}{x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan^{-1}x}{x} = 1$
    
4. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-\cos x}{x^2} = \frac{1}{2}$
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{2\sin^2(x/2)}{x^2} = 2\lim_{x \to 0} \left(\frac{\sin^2(x/2)}{(x/2)^2}\right) = 2 \times 1 = \frac{2 \times 1}{4} = \frac{1}{2}$

Q. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin \alpha x}{\sin \beta x}$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin \alpha x}{\alpha x} \times \alpha x$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin bx}{bx} \times bx = \frac{a}{b}$
    
Q. $\frac{1-\cos 4x}{x^2} = \frac{2\sin^2 2x}{x^2}$
    
    $2 \times \left(\frac{\sin 2x}{x}\right)^2$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} 2 \times \left(\frac{2\sin x \cos x}{x}\right)^2$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} 2 \times 4 = 8$
    
Q. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^2}{\sin^2 x}$  
    when interchanged the answer doesn't change
    
    $= 1$
    



Q.$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{2x\cos 2x - 3\sin 2x}{4x + \tan x}$
    
    div by $x$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{2\cos 2x - 3}{4+1} = \frac{4}{5}$
    
Q. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sec 5x - \sec 3x}{\sec 3x - \sec x}$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos 3x - \cos 5x}{\cos 3x \cos 5x} \times \frac{\cos x \cos 3x}{\cos x - \cos 3x}$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{-2\sin 4x \cos(-x)}{\cos 3x \cos 5x} \times \frac{\cos x \cos 3x}{-2\sin 2x \sin(-x)}$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 4x}{\sin 2x} \times \frac{2\sin 2x \cos 2x}{\sin 2x}$
    
    $\lim_{x \to 0} 2\cos 2x = 2$
