suppose we insert **n terms** between a and b then
$a,A_{1},A_{2},A_{3},A_{n},b$
if these form an A.P:
	number of terms = n+2
	$b = (n+2)th\; term$
		$b = T_{n+2}$
		$b=a+(n+1)d$
		$\boxed{d=\frac{b-a}{n+1}}$
	- $A_{n} = a+nd$

## Properties
- The sum of n arithmetic means between 2 numbers is n times the single A.M between them
	$A_{1}+A_{2}+A_{3}\dots A_{n}= n(A)$($A=\frac{a+b}{2}$)
