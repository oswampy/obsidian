 #Q 

a) Two students Anil and Ashima appeared in exam. Probability that Anil will qualify is $0.05$ probability that Ashima will qualify is $0.1$ Probability both will qualify is $0.02$. Find

i) **Probability that Both will not qualify**

- $P(E \cap F') = (E \cup F)' = 1 - E \cup F = 1 - 0.13 = 0.87$

ii) **At least one will not qualify** complement of both qualifying $= 1 - 0.02 = 0.98$

iii) **Only one will qualify** either Anil qualify or and Ashima don't or vice versa.


 $P(E \cup F') = P(E) - P(E \cap F) = 0.08$
 
 $P(F \cup E') = P(F) - P(F \cap E) = 6.03$
 
 $\Rightarrow 0.11$
