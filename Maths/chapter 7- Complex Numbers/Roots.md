**Roots of a Complex Number**

**Properties of $z^{1/n}$ roots:**

i) All roots of $z^{1/n}$ are in A.P. with $r=e^{2\pi i/n}$

ii) Sum of all roots of $z^{1/n}$ is always $0$

iii) Product of all roots of $z^{1/n}$ is $(-1)^{n-1}z$

iv) Modulus of all roots of $z^{1/n}$ are equal and each equal to $|z|^{1/n}$

v) Amplitude of all roots of $z^{1/n}$ are in A.P. with $d=\dfrac{2\pi}{n}$

vi) All roots of $z^{1/n}$ lie on the circumference of a circle whose centre is origin and radius is equal to $|z|^{1/n}$. Also these roots divide the circle into $n$ parts and form a polygon of $n$ sides.

## **Cube roots of unity**

Equation:  
$x^3 - 1 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 1^{1/3}$

General form:  
$x = (\cos 0 + i \sin 0)^{1/3}$

So,  
$x = \cos \dfrac{2k\pi}{3} + i \sin \dfrac{2k\pi}{3},  k = 0,1,2$

Roots are:  
$\cos \dfrac{2\pi}{3} + i \sin \dfrac{2\pi}{3}, \quad \cos \dfrac{4\pi}{3} + i \sin \dfrac{4\pi}{3}, \quad 1$

Values:  
$\omega = -\dfrac{1}{2} + i\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \quad \omega^2 = -\dfrac{1}{2} - i\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}, \quad 1$

---

**Properties of cube roots of unity**

1. $1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0$
    
2. $\omega^{3n} = 1$
    
3. $1 + \omega^n + \omega^{2n}$ =   
    0, if $n$ is not a multiple of 3
    3, if $n$ is a multiple of 3
