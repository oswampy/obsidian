- $i = \sqrt{-1}$
    
- $i^2 = -1$
    
- $i^3 = i^2 \cdot i = -i$
    
- $i^4 = i^2 \cdot i^2 = 1$

Q1) Expand $(i - 1)^4$
	$(i - 1)^4 = \big((i - 1)^2\big)^2 = (i^2 - 2i + 1)^2 = (-1 - 2i + 1)^2 = (-2i)^2 = -4$

Q2)Prove that $i^{53} + i^{72} + i^{93} + i^{102} = 2i$
	$i^{53}=i^{4\cdot13+1}=i,\quad i^{72}=i^{4\cdot18}=1,\quad i^{93}=i^{4\cdot23+1}=i,\quad i^{102}=i^{4\cdot25+2}=i^2=-1.$  
	$\Rightarrow i^{53}+i^{72}+i^{93}+i^{102}=i+1+i-1=2i.$

Q3)2. Prove $\Big(i^{21} - (i^{-1})^{46}\Big)^2 = 2i$
    
Simplify:  
	$i^{21} = i$, and $i^{-1} = -i$
	So, $(i - (-i)^{46})^2 = (i - ((-1)^{46} i^{46}))^2 = (i - i^2)^2 = (i + 1)^2 = 1 + 2i - 1 = 2i$
