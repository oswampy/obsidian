## Measurement of angles
$\pi$ radians = 180 degrees
1 radians = 57.3 degrees

converting degrees to radians:
$$\theta*\frac{\pi}{180}$$
converting radians to degrees:
$$\theta* \frac{180}{\pi}$$
Q) Find angle between minute and hour hand when time is 7:20
A) when the hour hand moves to 7 and the minute hand is approaching 4 the hour hand also moves a certain distance along with the minute hand, this distance is the same as the distance taken for it to reach 4 => 20/60 hrs which is 1/3 hrs so the hour hand moves 1/3 of the way to the NEXT hour(so 10 degrees) while the minute hand approaches 4. we know the distance between 4 and 7 is 90 degrees so the angle between them is 90 + 10 = 100 degrees
