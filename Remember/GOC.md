### for chlorination of alkenes

$1^o product\% = \frac{nprp}{nprp+nsrs+ntrt}*100$
$2^o product\% = \frac{nsrs}{nprp+nsrs+ntrt}*100$
$3^o product\% = \frac{ntrt}{nprp+nsrs+ntrt}*100$

$$rp:rs:rt = 1:3.8:5.2$$
where nprp = no of 1 degree hydrogen 
where nsrs = no of 2 degree hydrogen 
where ntrt = no of 3 degree hydrogen 
since all denominators are same we can just check each struc for nprp,nsrs,ntrt and major and minor will be chosen accordingly
