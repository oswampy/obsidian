$X = \frac{individual\;moles}{total\;moles}$

$X = \frac{n_{A}}{n_{A}+n_{B}}$


#Q solution has 46% w/w ethyl alcohol in water, find mole fraction of c2h5oh

A) 
- mass of solution = 46g (46g in 100g of water)
- number of moles = 46/46 = **1**
- remaining mass is 54 g of water
- number of moles of water = 54/18 = 3
=>$X = \frac{1}{1+3} = 0.25$


#Trick
converting molality to mole fraction:
$$X_{solute} = \frac{m*M_{2}}{1000+(m*M_{2})}$$ ($M_{2}$ is molar mass of solvent$)
