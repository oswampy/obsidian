what are they? 
moles are basically the amount of particles in 6.022 x $10^{23}$ particles of a substance 
- example -> one mole of carbon is 6.022 x $10^{23}$ atoms of carbon (12g)
- one mole of an element is just its atomic weight
- one mole of CO2 has 6.022 x $10^{23}$ molecules in it
they are also called Avogadro's number ($N_A$)
-> you can imagine it like how one dozen is 12 items, one mole is 6.022 x $10^{23}$ items.

In chemistry no of moles is:
- n = Volume of gas / 22.4 L  (STP)
- n= Given Mass/ Molar Mass
- n= No of particles / Avogadros Number
