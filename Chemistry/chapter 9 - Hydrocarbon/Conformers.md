## Melting point:

M.P of Cis is slightly lower than trans because trans is more symmetrical, has better packing, Per lattice energy.

## Boiling Point:

B.P of cis is slightly Per than trans because cis has $\uparrow$ magnitude of dipole moment (ionic nature $\uparrow$)

### Stability of Alkenes

$\Rightarrow$ Presence of Alkyl group on the double bonded C-atom $\uparrow$ the stability of Alkene.

Reason: Hyperconjugation, more Alkyl group more $\alpha$-H

- Structure (II) with $H$ and $CH_3$ groups
