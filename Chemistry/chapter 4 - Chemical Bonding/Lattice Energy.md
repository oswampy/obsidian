## Factors on which lattice energy depends
$\text{Na}^{+} + \text{Cl}^{-} \rightarrow \text{NaCl} + \text{L.E}$

$F = \dfrac{k q_{1} q_{2}}{r^{2}} \quad (\text{electrostatic force of attraction})$
1 ) $\uparrow F \Rightarrow \uparrow \text{L.E}$

2 ) $\text{L.E} \propto z^{+} \text{ and } z^{-} \quad (\text{charges})$

3 ) $\text{Size of ions } (r) \propto \dfrac{1}{\text{L.E}}$

### Q)$\text{L.E in increasing order:}$
A)
1)$\text{NaF} < \text{MgF}_{2} < \text{AlF}_{3} \quad (\text{Size not considered, only charge})$

2)$\text{Na}_{2}\text{O} < \text{MgO} < \text{Al}_{2}\text{O}_{3} \quad (\text{O}^{2-} \text{ same, so charge on cation})$

3)$\text{Li}_{2}\text{O} < \text{Li}_{3}\text{N} \quad (\text{N}^{3-} > \text{O}^{2-})$

4)$\text{NaCl} > \text{KCl} \quad (\text{size})$

5)$\text{NaF} < \text{MgCl}_{2} \quad (\text{same charge but size } \text{Cl}^{-} > \text{F}^{-})$

$\textbf{Note:}$ When there is a tie between charge and size, we always prefer charge. 
	Size is measured in picometers, but charge goes:  
	$+1 \rightarrow +2 \rightarrow +3$

## Factors affecting polarization

### **Cation:**

$в†‘$ Charge so $в†‘$ Polarizing power so $в†‘$ Covalent character
