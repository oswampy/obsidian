# Types of Chemical Bonds
In order of general bond strength
1) **Inter Atomic (strong)** 
	-Ionic Bond
	-Coordinate/dative Bond
	-Co-valent Bond
	-Metallic Bond
	Bond Dissociation Energy -> 200-400 kJ/mole
2) **Inter Molecular (weak)**
	-Hydrogen Bond
	-Van Der Waal Bond
	Bond Dissociation Energy -> 8-40 kJ/mole

# Reason for Chemical Bonds
 To achive octet rule (duplet is an exception)
	Noble gas configuration for stability as it has lowest P.E
	 $\downarrow P.E=\uparrow stability$
