# Balancing by Ion Electron Method


**Rules:**

1. Write O.N.'s of all atoms above their symbols on each equation
2. Find out species which are getting oxidized and reduced
3. Split the whole equation into 2 half reactions i.e oxidation half reaction and Reduction half reaction
4. While balancing each half reaction add e⁻ to which ever side
    - if necessary in order to make up for difference in oxidation No's
