
## Basic Concepts
Redox titration in which titrant and analyte undergo redox reactions and end point is detected by colour change to find the strength of analyte.

**Titrant:** Solution taken in burette whose concentration is known

**Analyte:** Solution taken in conical flask whose concentration is unknown

## Indicators


### Need for Indicators

At the equivalence point, amount of oxidant is equal to amount of reductant.

To detect the point visually, an indicator that changes colour during redox reaction is used.
