-> A node is a region in an atomic orbital where the probability of finding and electron is 0

- Types of Nodes:
1) Radial/Spherical Node
	they occur at certain radii
	formula for no of radial nodes is (n-l-1)
2) Angular/Planar/Conical Node
	they are regions where electron probability is 0 due to angular(directional) components of wavefunction
	formula is just L(azimuthal quantum number) 

**Total number of nodes = n-1**

Bohr's Bury Rule(n+l rule):
to find which orbital has more energy just add the n and l values, in case of a tie(degenerate orbitals), the one with higher n value has higher energy
	ex- 3s,2p -> i)3+0=2+1->3s>2p
