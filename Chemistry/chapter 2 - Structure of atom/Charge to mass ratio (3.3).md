JJ Thomson determined the ratio of electrical charge($e$) to mass of electron ($m_{e}$) by using cathode ray tube under the influence of electrical and magnetic fields which were placed perpendicular to eachother

He determined that the amount of deviation of the particles from their path in presence of the fields depends on factors like:
- **Magnitude**- greater the magnitude greater is the deviation
- **Mass**- less the mass of the particle,greater the deviation
- **Strength of the field**- the deflection of electrons from their path increases with increasing voltage across electrodes or magnetic field strength

by measuring deviations he was able to calculate the charge to mass ratio:
$$\frac{e}{m_{e}}=1.75 *10^{11}C\;kg^{-1}$$
