Difference between E.A and E.N:

**Electron Affinity:**
- Accepting Electrons
- Isolated Atom
- Absolute Term
- unit: kJ/mole
*---------------------------------------*
**Electronegativity:**

- Attraction of electrons
- Covalent bond
- Relative term
- No unit


## Factors on which E.N depends

1) Size $\propto$ $\frac{1}{E.N}$

2) Nuclear Charge $\propto$ E.N

3) Charge of cation $\propto$ E.N

4) Hybridisation ($\uparrow$ %s-character, $\uparrow$ E.N)
	$3p$- 50%, $sp^2$ = 33.33%, $sp^3$ = 25%
